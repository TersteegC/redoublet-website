About Redoublet.com

Redoublet.com was founded by Quirinus (father) and Christiaan Tersteeg (son). Our ambition with Redoublet.com is to provide a unique app that allows people to analyse their physical bridge game and see what was the optimal play. Later we will also launch online play options.
