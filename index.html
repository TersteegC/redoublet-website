About Redoublet.com

Redoublet.com was founded in 2023 by Quirinus (father) and Christiaan Tersteeg (son). Our ambition with Redoublet.com is to provide a user-friendly and fun alternative to the current online bridge offerings.

Our website offers unique features, including a variety of competitions and playing options. In just a few months, we expect to launch our app for both iOS and Android. Stay tuned!

If you have any feedback for us or would like to collaborate with us, don't hesitate to reach out.

Enjoy the game!
